Robot technology that results in an amazing wow-factor

The world´s first 3D printing technology for drinkable liquids

The technology merges methods from robotics, life sciences and design to explore a completely new field of 3D-printing

Rather than building up objects layer by layer, the process uses a high-end industrial robot to accurately inject microliter-drops of edible liquid into a cocktail or other liquid

Within 30 seconds, PRINT A DRINK can build up complex 3D structures in a wide range of drinks – creating fascinating augmented drinks using only natural ingredients
